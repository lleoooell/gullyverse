Who the fuck is gully don ?

Gully don is the leader of the first ghetto in the metaverse.

He’s the one who take good care of his community and make sure everyone have food on their table. He’s also the one who help the people to survive in the rough & rude living conditions in the ghetto

There is 4111 unique gully don nft living in the etherum blockchain and in the metaverse

Each nft give you access to our gully don’s exclusive club and also to the gullyverse, the first ghetto in the metaverse.

What is the gully don’s exclusive club ?

Backed by several trips in the heart of the ghetto and at the crossroads of reggae & dancehall and many other musical cultures, the gully don’s team had the idea to link its experience of the ghetto as well as the innovative web 3.0 ecosystem and the etherum blockchain.

It’s time for the gully dons to unite, join forces and make sure all people in the ghetto can fulfill their dreams and that’s the reason why the team created the gully don’s exclusive club.

The gully don’s exclusive club is inspired by the evolution of the worldwide ghettos from where everything started : visions, music, culture, history...

By holding a gully don nft you will be able to enter the club, enter the gullyverse and become a part of its evolution.

What is the Gullyverse ?

Welcome to the gullyverse, the first ghetto in the metaverse.

The gully don’s exclusive club members will be allow to enter the first ghetto in the metaverse, a full 3d environment created by our development team.

Members will attend some exclusive events among others : live. Concerts, sound systems shows, meeting with some of the top artist on the scene...

Main Goal & Ambition

Create a tough community of owners of one of our nfts and united around one main idea:

Support the ghetto, its initiatives, its talents while having fun and become a member of the gully don’s exclusive club.

Our ambition is to support some official associations of our choice to regularly pay them funds made available by the gully don’s exclusive club and which will be used to implement different positive actions directly in the ghetto (south sudan, jamaica, french caribbean...)
